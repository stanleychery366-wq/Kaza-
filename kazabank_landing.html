The Problem

Financial exclusion
  • Over 70% of Haitians are unbanked.
  • High remittance fees and slow cash-out.
Payment barriers
  • Limited access to international e-commerce.
  • Cash dependency and security risks.
Operational friction
  • Long queues for basic withdrawals.
  • Low merchant acceptance and tools.

Our Solution

Cards (virtual + physical)
  • Reloadable prepaid and debit cards.
  • Instant virtual issuance; optional physical card.
Reload & cash-in
  • MonCash, NatCash, and POS agents.
  • Low fees and fast settlement.
Acceptance
  • Local QR (e.g., ParyajPam) and global POS/e‑commerce.
  • Tokenization for Apple/Google/Samsung Pay.
Security & control
  • EMV, tokenization, 3‑D Secure.
  • Spend controls, PIN, block/unblock.

Why Mastercard

  • Digital First issuance and global acceptance.
  • Processing Core APIs for full card lifecycle management.
  • Advanced fraud prevention and compliance standards.
